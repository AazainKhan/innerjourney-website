Language evolved through human communication needs, gradually developing from basic sounds and gestures into complex systems of symbols and grammar.

Numerology is also a language, like any other language. Numerology and language share common elements in their structured systems of symbols and meanings. Both involve interpreting patterns to convey information and understanding. While language primarily communicates through words, numerology uses numbers to decode insights about life and the cyclic patterns of the universe that influence in our lives.

Numbers serve in our lives as powerful storytellers, unveiling the narratives of what lies ahead. They adeptly identify the strengths and weaknesses, opportunities and challenges, guiding us toward a deeper understanding of ourselves and the path. It helps to embark on the journey of self-discovery, a powerful tool that holds the key to unlocking our spiritual growth. Through practical methods, numbers reveal the very core of our being, uncovering innate talents, deepest aspirations, and hidden traits.

Numbers can be seen as archetypes. They represent qualities that all of us possess in greater or lesser quantities. Each number exhibits archetypal quality. Let me throw a brief light on this.

## Number 1

Number 1 is the most independent, unconventional, and individualistic by nature. It represents the energy of the Sun. Number 1 is masculine and dynamic. It is courageous and ambitious.

## Number 2

Number 2 is the gentlest of all the numbers representing passiveness and cooperation. It's a feminine number. A number that represents the Moon. It is a number of a peacemaker.

## Number 3

Number 3 represents wisdom and joy. It is a number of motivations. The planet that is associated with the number 3 is Jupiter.

## Number 4

Number 4 is a practical number by nature. It is orderly, methodical, and firm. Depending on association with other numbers it exhibits and changes its character. Number 4 is associated with the shadowy planet Rahu / North Lunar Node.

## Number 5

Number 5 is the most dynamic of all numbers. It represents childish traits and adapts according to the situation and associations. Number 5 represents the planet Mercury.

## Number 6

Number 6 is the most loving of all the numbers. It is a number representing luxury and comfort. Associated with the planet Venus — the Guru of happiness.

## Number 7

Number 7 is spiritual. It is the seeker of truth, and is analytical, contemplative, and meditative. It represents the South Lunar Node.

## Number 8

Number 8 is a number that is result-oriented driven. It is slow in giving rewards, but at the right time, it showers with the best of results. This number represents strength and the perseverance to see things through. Associated with the planet Saturn.

## Number 9

Number 9 is the number of commander-in-chief. A number that beholds the humanitarian traits at its best. Aggressive yet passionate in nature. Mars rules number 9.

## Decoding your numbers

By decoding the significance of the numbers present in our date of birth and name, we can gain insight into the life patterns that we follow, whether intentionally or unintentionally. And alter the results if unfavourable for us to grow and become a better human in the journey of life.
