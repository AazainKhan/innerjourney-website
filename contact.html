FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about my coaching process and services.

How does the Consultation work?

The Consultation is a 30-minute call where we'll discuss your goals, challenges, and how coaching can help you. There's no pressure or sales pitch - it's simply an opportunity for us to see if we're a good fit to work together.

What happens during a typical coaching session?

Each 60-minute session is tailored to your needs. We'll typically review your progress, address current challenges, work on specific strategies or tools, and set action steps for the week ahead. Sessions are conducted via video call or in-person if you're in the San Francisco area.

How long does it take to see results?

While everyone's journey is unique, most clients begin to see positive changes within the first 3-4 weeks. Significant transformation typically occurs within 3-6 months of consistent coaching work.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, I offer flexible payment options for all my coaching packages. We can discuss a payment plan that works for your budget during our consultation call.

What if I need to reschedule a session?

Life happens! I ask for at least 24 hours notice for rescheduling. Sessions cancelled with less notice may be forfeited, but I'm always flexible for genuine emergencies.
